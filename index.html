Hello,

I'm Tammy and I am a Javascript/Node.js developer! I began learning to code in early 2017 after a long while of being interested, but never making the jump. I began with Java working through Codecademy & Udacity and worked on a few small projects before switching to HTML, CSS & Javascript. I have since taken part in the London developer training program, Founders and Coders, which was an amazing experience.

I graduated from California State University, Fresno with a Bachelor's degree in Deaf Studies and worked as a Sign Language Interpreter for 3 years. I learned and experienced the importance of accessibility and how the deaf & deaf-blind communities often feel overlooked and ignored. As a result, I highly value accessibility and creating tech that is mindful of the user experience for people from all walks of life. I am continually using this webpage as an opportunity to implement and familiarize myself with accessibility practices, putting into practice things like ARIA attributes, customizable font size, strong, descriptive alt-text as well as utilizing Chromevox and other tools like Lighthouse and aXe for auditing the page.

This gallery is a place for me to share photos I've taken that I enjoy. Please browse, click and hover to learn more about me and the photos you see on this page. You can also refresh to see different images. Enjoy!
