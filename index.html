ABOUT

HELLO,

I'm Tammy and I am a self-taught programming “student”. I started on this path in March of 2017. I am interested in front-end web development, but I have definite plans to expand my skills to the Full Stack. At this point, I have some experience in Javascript, Java and HTML/CSS.

I graduated from California State University, Fresno with a bachelor's degree in Communicative Disorders and Deaf Studies. I worked as a Sign Language Interpreter for 3 years and had some interaction with the deaf-blind community as well. In my experiences with these communities, I have learned a bit regarding the importance of accessibility and how these communities often feel overlooked and ignored. I feel that I can bring some of my knowledge into my work and with future teams by being an advocate for marginalized groups that are sometimes forgotten.

This gallery is a place for me to display some of the photos I've taken over the last few years that I'm proud of. Please browse, click and hover to learn more about me and the photos you see on this page. You can also refresh to see different images. Enjoy!
