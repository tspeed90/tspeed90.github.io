Hello,

I'm Tammy! I am a Software Developer and California native currently living in London. I am also a graduate of the London-based developer training program, Founders and Coders. I had an incredible time learning in a collaborative environment and I'm extremely grateful for the experience!

In my pre-coding life, I graduated from California State University, Fresno with a degree in Deaf Studies and worked as a Sign Language Interpreter for 3 years in universities. As a result of my experiences as an interpreter, I highly value accessibility and creating tech that is mindful of the user experience for people from all walks of life. I continue to use this webpage as an opportunity to implement and familiarize myself with accessibility practices, putting into practice things like ARIA attributes, customizable font size, strong, descriptive alt-text as well as utilizing Chromevox and other tools like Lighthouse and aXe for running audits.

Some of my hobbies include cooking, baking, attending meetups, learning new things (coding and non-coding related), going on holiday, and taking photos (often on said holidays).
